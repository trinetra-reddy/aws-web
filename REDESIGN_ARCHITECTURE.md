**Type**: GPU-accelerated, 60fps

- **Text Reveal**: Staggered fade-in with slide

**Libraries**:
- Framer Motion (React animations)
- Three.js (3D graphics - lazy loaded)
- GSAP (complex sequences)
- Lottie (vector animations)

### Micro-Interactions
- Button hover effects (scale, glow)
- Card lift on hover
- Smooth page transitions
- Loading states
- Success animations
- Error shake animations
- Progress indicators

### Scroll Animations
- Fade in on scroll
- Slide in from sides
- Number counters
- Progress bars
- Parallax backgrounds
- Sticky elements

**Performance Rules**:
- Use `transform` and `opacity` only
- Avoid layout thrashing
- Lazy load heavy animations
- Reduce motion for accessibility
- 60fps minimum
- Mobile-optimized

## 🤖 AI Chatbot Specifications

### Chatbot Personality
**Name**: "Ava" (Anantha Virtual Assistant)
**Tone**: Professional, helpful, knowledgeable
**Avatar**: Modern AI icon with animation

### Conversation Flows

#### Flow 1: General Inquiry
```
Ava: Hi! I'm Ava, your AI assistant. How can I help you today?
User: [Types question]
Ava: [Provides answer + related resources]
Ava: Would you like to speak with a specialist about this?
User: Yes
Ava: Great! Let me collect a few details...
[Lead capture form]
```

#### Flow 2: Service Inquiry
```
Ava: I see you're interested in [Service]. What's your main goal?
User: [Describes goal]
Ava: Based on that, I'd recommend [Solution].
Ava: Would you like a free consultation to discuss this?
[Calendar booking or form]
```

#### Flow 3: Pricing Inquiry
```
Ava: I can help with pricing! Which service are you interested in?
User: [Selects service]
Ava: What's your project timeline and budget range?
User: [Provides info]
Ava: Based on that, here's an estimate... [Shows range]
Ava: For an accurate quote, let's schedule a quick call.
[Lead capture]
```

### Chatbot Features
- **Context Awareness**: Remembers conversation history
- **Smart Routing**: Directs to right team member
- **Multilingual**: English, Hindi (expandable)
- **Business Hours**: Shows availability
- **Offline Mode**: Captures message for follow-up
- **File Sharing**: Can receive/send documents
- **Screen Sharing**: For technical support
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Detects frustration, escalates
